❓ How to Play

Capture the most land. Here's all you need to know:

1
Steer with the arrow keys or WASD. You always move forward and turn in 90° angles. On phone, swipe a direction.
2
Leave your home to draw a trail. The moment you step off your own colored land, a trail follows behind you.
3
Loop back home to claim land. Return to your territory and everything your loop encloses becomes yours. Surround an enemy's land to take it all.
4
Don't get cut. If anyone touches your trail — including yourself — you lose your land. Cut a rival's trail and you steal theirs. Walls are safe; you slide along them.
5
Win the board. Climb the leaderboard, earn coins from kills and big captures, spend them in the Shop on power-ups (tap SHIFT to use Speed Boost), and try Battle Royale for one-life survival.
